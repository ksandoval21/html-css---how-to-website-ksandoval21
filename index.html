Painting101

Easy steps to become an artist!

  1. Decide on a design you want to paint

    2. You can get inspiration off the internet or free-style.

  2. Pick out the kind of paint you want to use.

    3. Figure out the colors you want to use.

  3. Collect brushes
    • If you have a more detailed design choose thinner brushes.
  4. Pick a canvas

    5. Decide the best size canvas for your specific design.

  5. Get to work

    6. Get creative
